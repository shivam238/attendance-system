What is QR Attendance System?

QR Attendance System is a cutting-edge web application designed specifically for educational institutions to manage student attendance digitally. By leveraging QR code technology and cloud-based storage, we've created a seamless experience that benefits both educators and students.

The platform enables Class Representatives (CRs) to generate unique QR codes for each class session, which students can scan using their smartphones to instantly mark their attendance. All data is synchronized in real-time through Google Firebase, ensuring accuracy and reliability.

Why We Built This

Traditional attendance methods involve several challenges:

  • Time-Consuming: Manual roll calls waste valuable class time
  • Error-Prone: Handwritten registers are susceptible to mistakes and tampering
  • Storage Issues: Physical registers require space and deteriorate over time
  • Difficult Analysis: Extracting insights from paper records is tedious
  • Environmental Impact: Significant paper consumption

QR Attendance System addresses all these pain points with a simple, elegant solution that works on any device with an internet connection.

How It Works

For Class Representatives

  1. Register: Create an account with your class details, subjects, and student list
  2. Generate QR: Select a subject and generate a unique QR code for the session
  3. Display: Show the QR code in class or share the link via WhatsApp/email
  4. Monitor: Watch real-time attendance updates as students mark their presence
  5. Download: Export attendance reports in Excel format anytime

For Students

  1. Scan: Use your smartphone to scan the QR code displayed by your CR
  2. Enter Roll Number: Type your roll number exactly as registered
  3. Submit: Click submit and receive instant confirmation
  4. Done: Your attendance is recorded and synced in real-time

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes! QR Attendance System is completely free to use with no hidden charges, subscription fees, or premium tiers.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required! It's a web-based application that works directly in your browser.

What if I lose my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" feature on the login page. You'll receive an OTP on your registered mobile number to reset your password.

Can students mark attendance from home?

QR codes are time-limited (10 minutes) to prevent misuse. However, students technically can mark from anywhere if they have the link. CRs should monitor attendance timing to ensure authenticity.

What happens if my internet disconnects?

The system will show an "Offline" status. Once reconnected, data will sync automatically. We recommend stable internet for best experience.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can permanently delete your account and all data using the "Delete My Account" option in the guide modal (accessible via "i" button).

Is my data safe?

Yes! We use Google Firebase for secure cloud storage with encryption. Your data is protected and never shared with third parties.

Can I use this for multiple classes?

Currently, each CR account is designed for one class. You can manage multiple subjects within that class. For multiple classes, you'd need separate accounts.
