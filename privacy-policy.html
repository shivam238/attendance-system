Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 18, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Welcome to QR Attendance System. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our attendance management platform. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Registration Data: When you register as a Class Representative, we collect your passout year, college name, branch, section number, mobile number, and password.
  • Student Information: Names and roll numbers of students added to the system by the CR.
  • Subject Data: Subject names and codes for attendance tracking.
  • Attendance Records: Date, time, subject, and student attendance status.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform, and interaction patterns.
  • Connection Data: Firebase connection status and database synchronization logs.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We use Google Firebase for data storage and real-time synchronization. Firebase may collect additional technical information as outlined in Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our attendance management system.
  • Authentication: To verify user identity and secure account access.
  • Data Management: To store, organize, and retrieve attendance records.
  • Communication: To send important updates, security alerts, and system notifications.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve system performance.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security issues.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Your Data

All data is securely stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database, a cloud-based NoSQL database service. Firebase servers are located in data centers that comply with international security standards.

3.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Access Controls: Firebase security rules restrict unauthorized data access.
  • Authentication: Password-protected accounts with optional OTP verification.
  • Session Management: Time-limited QR codes (10-minute expiry) prevent unauthorized attendance marking.
  • Regular Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities and security threats.

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage users to maintain strong passwords and report any suspicious activity immediately.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Disclosure Scenarios

We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulations.
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.
  • Service Providers: To trusted third-party service providers (like Firebase) who assist in operating our platform under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

5.1 What We Use

  • Session Storage: Temporary storage for maintaining user login sessions.
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve system performance.
  • Google AdSense (if applicable): Advertising cookies for personalized ad delivery.

5.2 Your Choices

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. To opt out of personalized advertising, visit Google Ad Settings.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the system
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • View attendance records and history

6.2 Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data using the "Delete My Account" feature. This action:

  • Requires password and OTP verification
  • Permanently removes all student records
  • Deletes all attendance history
  • Cannot be undone

6.3 Data Portability

You can download your attendance data in Excel format at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for college students and educational administrators. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware of such collection, we will take immediate steps to delete the information.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account at any time. After deletion, data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States (where Firebase servers are located). These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites (e.g., Instagram, LinkedIn). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect:

  • Changes in our practices
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • New features or services

When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify users through the system or via email
  • Provide a summary of changes

12. Your Consent

By using our QR Attendance System, you consent to:

  • The collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy
  • The storage of your data in Google Firebase
  • The processing of your data for attendance management purposes
