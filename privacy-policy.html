Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 18, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Welcome to QR Attendance System. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our attendance management platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide (registration data, student lists, subject data) and technical data (IP address, browser type) to provide and improve our service.

2. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database. We use encryption and access controls to protect your information.

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage users to maintain strong passwords and report any suspicious activity immediately.
