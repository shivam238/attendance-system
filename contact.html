Common Questions

How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, click "Forgot Password". Enter your username, verify the OTP sent to your registered mobile, and set a new password.
Is my data secure?
Yes! We use Firebase's secure cloud infrastructure with real-time encryption to protect all your records and account information.
Can I export attendance to Excel?
Absolutely. Go to the "History" tab on your dashboard and click "Export to Excel" to download a perfectly formatted report.
