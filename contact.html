We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.

Use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Please include details about what happened, what you expected, and steps to reproduce the issue.

💡 Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions. Select "Feature Request" in the form and describe your idea. We consider all feedback for future updates.

🤝 Are you open to collaboration?

Your contact information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We never share, sell, or use your information for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for details.

We're based in India (IST - Indian Standard Time, UTC+5:30). However, we respond to messages regardless of timezone.
