Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Data Collection & Device Fingerprinting

We collect only the information necessary to provide the attendance service. This includes student names, roll numbers, and attendance timestamps.

Anti-Proxy Device Fingerprinting: To prevent attendance fraud, our system generates and stores a persistent 3-layer Device ID on the student's device using localStorage, sessionStorage, and browser cookies. This allows the system to identify if multiple attendance submissions are being marked from the same device (proxying). This data is processed securely and is used solely for audit logs visible to the Class Representative (CR).

Location & Geofencing Data

When geofencing is enabled by the Class Representative (CR), our system requests access to the student device's GPS/location data to verify classroom presence. Important Notice: Location coordinates (latitude and longitude) are processed locally on the client-side to calculate distance from the center point, and are not stored permanently in our database. Only the calculated distance (in meters) and the out-of-range status are saved along with the pending request for CR verification.

Device Notifications

Our app requests user permission to display local push notifications. These notifications are used to confirm successful attendance marking, alert the CR of proxy activities, and provide other real-time app status updates. No personal data is transmitted through local notifications.

Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively for generating attendance reports and managing class records within the application. We do not sell or share this data with third parties.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including Google Firebase authentication and database rules, to ensure that your data is accessed only by authorized users.

User Control

Users have the right to delete their accounts and all associated data at any time through the in-app settings. Deletion is permanent and removes all records from our database.
