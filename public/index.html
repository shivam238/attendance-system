The Story of AttenMo

AttenMo was originally known as Attendify, first developed in 2025 by Shivam Kumar Mahto — at the suggestion of one of his CR friends who needed a better way to handle attendance.

🚀 What began as a small solution for a specific problem was extensively redesigned and generalized over time — adding broader features, improving usability, and building a flexible system useful for both students and teachers.

⏳ This complete evolution from Attendify → AttenMo took approximately 10 months of continuous development and refinement.
