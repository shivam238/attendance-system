About the System

Our Mission

ATTENDIFY was born from the need for a modern, efficient, and transparent way to track student attendance. We aim to replace manual registers and tedious roll calls with a seamless digital experience that benefits both teachers and students.

How it Works

Teachers generate a dynamic, time-limited QR code for their subject. Students simply scan the code using their mobile devices, and their attendance is instantly recorded in our secure cloud database. No more proxy attendance or manual entry errors.

🚀 Fast & Seamless

Marks attendance in seconds with a simple scan.

📊 Smart Reporting

Export detailed attendance reports to Excel instantly.

🔐 Highly Secure

Data is protected using Firebase's enterprise-grade security.

📱 Mobile Ready

Optimized for all smartphones with zero app install required.

📍 Geofencing

Prevents fake attendance by verifying students are physically present within the classroom boundary.

🆓 100% Free

No subscriptions, no hidden fees. Built on open-source tools for everyone.
